About

This Classroom Finder searches in a database we fill every hour for any unscheduled classrooms on campus and presents the information in a sorted, meaningful way. Simply whip out your smartphone while wandering around on campus and you can instantly find a quiet, comfortable room to actually get work done. The dropdown will give a better idea of what to expect and the link will bring you to actual images of the room.
